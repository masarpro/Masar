---
title: Employees & Payroll
description: Managing HR — employee records, payroll runs, leave, assets and custody, and company expenses.
---

## Employee records

From the **Company** section, create a record for every employee: personal details, salary and allowances, and documents (residency, contract, certificates) with expiry alerts.

## Payroll runs

1. Create a **payroll run** for the month — it's prepared automatically with all employees, salaries, allowances, and deductions.
2. Review and adjust as needed (deductions, bonuses, overtime).
3. Approve the run — salary and social insurance journal entries are posted automatically.

## Leave

Leave requests of all types (annual, sick, unpaid...) with an approval workflow and a leave balance per employee.

## Assets and custody

- Register company assets: vehicles, equipment, devices.
- Hand them to employees as **custody** and track their condition and return.

## Project assignments

Assign employees to projects for specific periods — so you know who works where, and labor cost is charged to the right project in profitability reports.

## Company expenses

Record general administrative expenses (rent, subscriptions, utilities) separately from project expenses, and enable **recurring expenses** to be logged automatically every month.
