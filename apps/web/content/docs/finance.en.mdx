## E-invoicing (ZATCA)

Masar invoices comply with **Phase 2** of Saudi e-invoicing (integration):

- Cryptographic signing and direct integration with ZATCA.
- A **QR code** on every invoice.
- Automatic **15% VAT** calculation.
- Credit notes for adjusting issued invoices.

Create an invoice from **Finance → Invoices**, link it to a project and client, and issue it — issued invoices cannot be edited, preserving tax compliance.

## Expenses

Record any expense from the unified **Add Expense** dialog (available from the dashboard, finance section, and project page):

- **General expense** — materials, labor, equipment, transport... with ready hierarchical categories.
- **Subcontract payment** — linked to a subcontractor contract.
- **Owner drawing** — partner withdrawals.

Attach a photo of the receipt and link the expense to a project so it's automatically deducted from its profitability.

## Payments and collection

- Record **client payments** against invoices — bank balance and client account update automatically.
- Track **receivables aging**: who owes you, how much, and since when.

## Receipt and payment vouchers

- A **receipt voucher** for every amount in, a **payment voucher** for every amount out — with automatic numbering and an approval cycle.
- Professional Arabic printing with the amount written in words.
- Every voucher automatically reflects on accounting and bank accounts.

## Bank accounts and cash boxes

- Add all your bank accounts and cash boxes and track each balance in real time.
- Record **transfers** between accounts.
- Use **bank reconciliation** to match your bank statement against your records.

## Financial reports

A comprehensive finance dashboard and ready reports: project profitability, expenses by category, cash flow, receivables aging, and VAT — all exportable to Excel and PDF.
