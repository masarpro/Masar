## What is the owner portal?

The owner portal lets the project owner follow their project through a secure link you send them — **no registration, no account**. It builds trust with your clients and saves you the "what's happening with my project?" calls.

## Activation

1. From the project page, open the **Owner Portal** tab.
2. Click **Activate Portal** and choose what the owner sees.
3. Copy the secure link and send it to the owner (WhatsApp, email, message).

## What does the owner see?

You control every element in detail:

- Project progress and milestones.
- Site photos and updates.
- Recorded and due payments.
- Documents you choose to share.

## Owner chat

The owner can message you directly from their portal through the **owner channel** in project chat — keeping all communication documented in one place.

## Security

- The link is encrypted and unguessable.
- You can **revoke the link** at any moment or generate a new one.
- The owner sees only what you allow — nothing else.
