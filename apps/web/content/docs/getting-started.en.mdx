## Creating an account

1. From the homepage, click **Start for free**.
2. Sign up with your email and a password, or directly with a Google account.
3. Confirm your email from the activation message.

The free plan requires no credit card and includes a full project, two users, and 10 AI assistant conversations.

## Setting up your organization

After your first sign-in, the setup wizard walks you through:

1. **Organization name** — your company name as it will appear on invoices and reports.
2. **Tax details** — VAT number and commercial registration (required for e-invoicing).
3. **Logo & branding** — upload your logo to appear on invoices, quotations, and printouts.

You can edit all of this later from **Settings**.

## Inviting your team

From **Settings → Members**, invite team members by email and pick a role for each:

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| Manager | Everything except deletion and sensitive settings |
| Accountant | Finance, invoices, and vouchers |
| Engineer | Projects, execution, and quantity takeoff |
| Field Supervisor | Daily reports and site photos |
| Viewer | Read-only |

You can also create custom roles with fine-grained per-section permissions.

## Your first project

1. From the dashboard, click **New Project**.
2. Enter the project name, client, and contract value.
3. Click **Create** — you'll get a full project page: overview, finance, execution, documents, and more.

## Language and theme

- **Language:** from the language icon in the top bar — Arabic or English.
- **Theme:** light or dark from the moon/sun icon.
