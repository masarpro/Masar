## What does the assistant do?

The Masar AI assistant is available from the floating button on every page. It answers from **your own data** in natural language:

- "How much did I spend on the villa project this month?"
- "Which claims are overdue?"
- "What's the Al Ahli bank account balance?"
- "Summarize the plumbing subcontractor's position"
- "What's the value of open quotations?"

## Page awareness

The assistant knows which page you're on — ask it while viewing an invoice and it understands the context, answers accordingly, and guides you to the right screen when needed.

## Permissions and privacy

- The assistant reads **only** your organization's data.
- It respects **the permissions of the user** asking — a field supervisor can't ask about payroll without permission.
- Your data is **not used** to train AI models.

## Saved conversations

Your conversations are saved automatically; revisit them from the chat history or start a new conversation anytime.

## Limits

- Free plan: **10 conversations** to try the assistant.
- Paid plans: **unlimited** conversations.
