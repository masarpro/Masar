## Available plans

| Plan | Price | Best for |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | 0 SAR — forever | Trying the system on a real project |
| **Monthly** | 199 SAR / month | Contractors and small offices |
| **Yearly** | 1,999 SAR / year (≈166 SAR/month) | Best value — save 389 SAR |
| **Enterprise** | 24,499 SAR setup + monthly subscription | 50+ users and full customization |

## What does each plan include?

**Free:** one project, two users, 10 AI conversations, and core project management.

**Paid (monthly/yearly):** unlimited projects, up to 9 users, unlimited AI conversations, PDF and Excel export, the owner portal, ZATCA e-invoicing, and all reports and analytics.

**Enterprise:** everything above + 50+ users, full customization, training, dedicated support, and a guaranteed SLA.

## Upgrading and downgrading

From **Settings → Subscription**:

- **Upgrades** apply immediately with automatic proration.
- **Downgrades** take effect from the next billing cycle.
- **Cancellation** is available anytime — your subscription stays active until the end of the paid period.

## Payment methods

Credit cards (Visa and Mastercard) with secure online payment. For enterprise: bank transfer and direct invoicing.

## What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data stays safe and is never deleted — your account moves to the free plan with its limits, and you can come back anytime and find everything as you left it.
